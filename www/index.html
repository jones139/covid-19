Teesside COVID-19 Tracker

The charts below show the data published by Public Health England for individual local authority areas around Teesside.

The first chart is just a representation of the historical data available on the Public Health England dashboard (their dashboard only shows a table, not a graph)

The second chart shows the data corrected for the population of the local authority areas to give a better comparison of the density of infections. The population data is obtained from the Mid Year Estimates published by the Office for National Statistics.

Remember Folks - Never make a judgement that relies on the last data point in a time series!

The data is processed using the Pandas data analysis library using the scipts published here. It should be updated every day automatically (if I have programmed my computer correctly.....) For any queries please email graham@openseizuredetector.org.uk
